Project Profile


Child Home


A Secure Home For Orphaned Girls

We started our Child Home Program from June, 2015. We have arranged seat for 10 Girl Child for now. It includes full security facilities at a secure zone and a 24/7 in-charge of the Child Home. The In-charge(female) has been recruited from the Village, who had no job there and no family either. So we could say that, this project has benefits for other people other than only Orphan children.
