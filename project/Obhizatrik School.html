Project Profile


Obhizatrik School


The Dream

From the beginning of the Foundation, We had a dream to pull off a school for the underprivileged children in our country. We thought of many ways to pull it off, knocked a lot of schools and colleges for a place to start. But for a long time we couldn’t find anything


How we started

In 2013, when we were still looking for a place for our school premise, with a coincidental event, we met Mr. Jahangir Alam, owner of Fame International School. When we proposed our dream project to him, he approved us to use his school premise for our school, which we named “Free School”. In 21st February, 2013, the journey of our Free School started with a grand orientation program for our new students. The journey of our “Free School” began and we never had to look back again.


Students of our Free School

We went to the slums of Duaripara and Bholay to talk to the kids and their parents there. For our first batch, we fixed our target of 20 students. In the slum, kids who couldn’t go to any school or don’t have the ability to afford school fees were our prime target.We were able to convince 25 families to send their kids to our school. This is how the dreams began for Alamin, Hridoy, Anwar, Lila and many more.


About Free School

Category: Underprivileged children
School Days: Saturday – Tuesday
Time: 3:30 PM – 5:30 PM
Counseling Day: Friday / Saturday


Course Plan

Pre Education: Discipline, Basic Education, Health & Nutrition, General Knowledge, Socialism
Primary Education: Class 1- 5
Higher Education: Class 6 – 10
Provided free equipments: Books, Khata, Bags, School Uniform
Instruments: Pencil/ Color pencil, Eraser, Sharpner, Oil Pastel, Dairy, Scale,
Rubik’s cube and other educational instruments Educational posters etc.


Monthly Health Check Up & Nutritious Food

We provide free health check-up for each of our students every month. We provide nutritious food to the students of Free School twice every week. We try to keep the tummy of our students in touch with every seasonal fruits.


Counseling with the parents on health & nutrition

We sit once every week with the parents and guardians of the students to counsel on the health, nutrition and improvement of the students.


Picnic & Study Tour

We included picnic as one of the entertainment source for the kids. Like Picnic to Shishu Park (2014). We plan on taking the students on study tour once every year. Like Visit to Mirpur Zoo (2013)


Games

Even though we cannot provide much space for the students to play, we try to arrange sports to participate for the students. Sports Day (2013)


Conclusion

After 1 whole year and a half, our students are in class 1. We are trying our best to give them a bright future, and at this point any kind of support from the society is welcome to us.
