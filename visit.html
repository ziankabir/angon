Our Location



Visitors from any part of bangladesh can come to Dhaka by Rail, Road , Waterways or Air. Then from any place in dhaka you can find bus for Mirpur-pallabi. You can also hire a CNG or Taxi. Please let us know before handhand about your visit by an email. email us

Visitors from other countries can come to Dhaka by Air. From Shahjalal International Airport you can hire a taxi and come to our office. If visiotrs want us to receive them from airport we can do it too. Please let us know before handhand about your visit by an email. email us
